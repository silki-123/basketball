Modules Overview

Shooting Analysis Module (Overview)

Goal:
  • Evaluate overall shooting technique.
Core Metrics:
  • Posture, trajectory, timing
  • Knee & hip angles
  • Elbow angle
  • Finger flexion
  • Ball entry angle, height, speed
  • Dribble & shot phase timing
Skilled Player:
  • Deep bend, high arc, fast release.
  • Smooth follow-through, steep entry angle.
  • Low stance, smooth hand switches, fast consistent rhythm.
Less Skilled Player:
  • Upright posture, flat arc, short release.
  • Straighter fingers, slower speed.
  • Upright, slow, irregular, weak control.
Outcome: Visual comparison, performance graphs, and trajectory mapping.

Dribbling Analysis Module (Overview)

Goal:
  • Measure ball handling and control efficiency.
Core Metrics:
  • Stance & posture
  • Dribble height (knee-waist optimal)
  • - Dribble frequency (bounces/sec)
  • Hand switching and transitions
  • - Ball proximity control
  • Dribble-to-action timing
Skilled Player:
  • Low stance, smooth hand switches, fast consistent rhythm.
Less Skilled Player:
  • - Upright, slow, irregular, weak control.

Output: Animation-based feedback and efficiency scores.

Trainer Feature: Assign personalized dribbling goals and drills, track progress.

Training & Exercise Tracking (Overview)

Goal:
  • Track physical strength, agility, and injury prevention.
Core Drills:
  • – Jumping (height, stability, takeoff/landing)
  • – Squats (depth, knee alignment)
  • – Lunges (stride, balance)
  • – Core (plank, twists)
  • – Push-ups (angle, range)
  • – Agility ladder drills (foot speed, stability)

Trainer Feature: Assign weekly exercise plans and evaluate completion scores.

Each generates data-driven feedback & progress tracking dashboards.
